Commonly Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is Aurelia Jewelry ethically sourced?

Yes. Aurelia is committed to responsible luxury. We use certified recycled gold and all our gemstones, including diamonds, are guaranteed conflict-free and ethically sourced to the highest global standards.

How long does standard shipping take?

Standard shipping within India typically takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping options (2-3 days) are available at checkout. You will receive a tracking number once your order is dispatched.

Can I return a personalized or engraved item?

Personalized or engraved items cannot be returned unless they arrive damaged or defective. Please review all customization details carefully before finalizing your order.

What metals are used in Aurelia's rings and bracelets?

We primarily use 18K solid gold, 14K rose gold, and rhodium-plated sterling silver. All our materials are hypoallergenic and nickel-free to ensure comfort and lasting quality.

Returns & Exchanges

30-Day Return Policy

We offer a hassle-free 30-day return policy from the date of delivery. Items must be in original condition with all tags and packaging intact.

How to Initiate a Return

  • Log into your Aurelia account
  • Navigate to "My Orders"
  • Select the item you wish to return
  • Choose a return reason and submit
  • Print the prepaid return label sent to your email

Exchange Policy

We accept exchanges for size adjustments and different styles within 30 days. The exchanged item must be of equal or greater value.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Personalized or engraved jewelry
  • Earrings (for hygiene reasons)
  • Items marked as final sale

Refund Timeline

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive your return. The amount will be credited to your original payment method.

Size Guide & Fit

Ring Sizing

To find your ring size, measure the circumference of your finger using a string or paper strip. Compare the measurement with our size chart:

  • Size 5: 49mm circumference
  • Size 6: 52mm circumference
  • Size 7: 55mm circumference
  • Size 8: 57mm circumference
  • Size 9: 60mm circumference

Bracelet Sizing

Measure your wrist at its widest point. Add 1-2cm for a comfortable fit:

  • Small: 15-17cm
  • Medium: 17-19cm
  • Large: 19-21cm

Necklace Length Guide

  • Choker (14-16"): Sits at the base of the neck
  • Princess (18"): Falls just below the collarbone
  • Matinee (20-24"): Sits at the center of the chest
  • Opera (28-34"): Falls below the bust line
