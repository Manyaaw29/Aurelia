Product Description

The Small Bar Bracelet blends sophistication with simplicity. Crafted from 18K gold-plated stainless steel mesh, it features a dainty circular charm symbolizing harmony and balance. Its secure magnetic clasp adds a touch of modern convenience while maintaining a seamless aesthetic. Designed to be waterproof and tarnish-resistant, this bracelet is ideal for daily wear or as a subtle accent for special occasions.
